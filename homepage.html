More about us:

We're very passionate in striving to improve the lives of others around, who are not able to make ends meet for themselves regardless of their socio-economic status. Challenges are always faced on a daily basis, hence the NGO's vision is to ease the livelihoods of the less privileged. We aim to create a space for the impoverished & enable them to make something of themselves from nothing.

GSP Governance:

iKhaya Lezizwe prides itself on good governance and is extremely appreciative to those who took their time to support the organization in so many ways.

Our Vision and mission:

Established in 2023 with the serious mission to help push the Global Agenda in promoting sustainable livelihood for the less privileged. The vision of our NGO is to support, and motivate the youth, by equipping them with fundamental skills of development skipped from early childhood.
